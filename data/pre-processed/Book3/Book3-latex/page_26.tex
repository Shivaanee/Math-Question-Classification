The rows of \(C\) are determined by the _mn_ scalars \(A_{ij}\) which are themselves the entries of an \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\). If (1-4) is expanded to

\[(C_{i1}\cdots C_{ip})=\sum_{r=1}^{n}\,(A_{ii}B_{r1}\cdots A_{ir}B_{rp})\]

we see that the entries of \(C\) are given by

\[C_{ij}=\sum_{r=1}^{n}\,A_{ir}B_{ri}.\]

**Definition**.: _Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix over the field \(F\) and let \(B\) be an \(n\times p\) matrix over \(F\). The_ **product**_\(AB\) is the \(m\times p\) matrix \(C\) whose \(i\), \(j\) entry is_

\[C_{ii}=\sum_{r=1}^{n}\,A_{ir}B_{ri}.\]
