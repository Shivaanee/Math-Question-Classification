**3.29**: For any \(A\), \(b\), \(x\), and \(y\), show that

1. if \(Ax=b\) and \(y^{\mathrm{T}}A=0\), then \(y^{\mathrm{T}}b=0\).
2. if \(Ax=0\) and \(A^{\mathrm{T}}y=b\), then \(x^{\mathrm{T}}b=0\).

What theorem does this prove about the fundamental subspaces?
