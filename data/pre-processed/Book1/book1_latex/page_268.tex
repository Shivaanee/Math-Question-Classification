**4.2**: If \(B=M^{-1}AM\), why is \(\det B=\det A\)? Show also that \(\det A^{-1}B=1\).

\[\begin{bmatrix}3&5\\ 6&9\end{bmatrix},\qquad\begin{bmatrix}\cos\theta&-\sin\theta\\ \sin\theta&\cos\theta\end{bmatrix},\quad\text{and}\quad\begin{bmatrix}a&b\\ a&b\end{bmatrix}.\]
